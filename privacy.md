 # Privacy Policy                                                                                                                                                                                                               
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 
  **Universal Church Supplies QBO Integration**                                                                                                                                                                                  
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 
  Last updated: February 7, 2026                                                                                                                                                                                                 
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 
  ## 1. Introduction                                                                                                                                                                                                             
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 
  This Privacy Policy describes how Universal Church Supplies Inc. handles data when using our internal QuickBooks Online integration.                                                                                           
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 
  ## 2. Data We Access                                                                                                                                                                                                           
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 
  This integration accesses QuickBooks Online data including:                                                                                                                                                                    
  - Customer records                                                                                                                                                                                                             
  - Invoices and bills                                                                                                                                                                                                           
  - Account information                                                                                                                                                                                                          
  - Vendor records                                                                                                                                                                                                               
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 
  ## 3. How We Use Data                                                                                                                                                                                                          
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 
  Data is used solely for internal business accounting and operations at Universal Church Supplies Inc.                                                                                                                          
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 
  ## 4. Data Sharing                                                                                                                                                                                                             
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 
  We do not sell, trade, or share QuickBooks data with third parties.                                                                                                                                                            
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 
  ## 5. Data Security                                                                                                                                                                                                            
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 
  Data is accessed via secure OAuth 2.0 authentication and transmitted over encrypted connections.                                                                                                                               
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 
  ## 6. Data Retention                                                                                                                                                                                                           
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 
  Data is accessed in real-time from QuickBooks Online. We do not store copies of QuickBooks data outside the QuickBooks platform.
